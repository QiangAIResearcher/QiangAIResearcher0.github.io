Short Bio

Mr. Qiang Zhang started his PhD research under supervision of Dr. Emine Yilmaz from September 2017. Before that, he obtained his bachelor and master degree from Shandong Univeristy in 2014 and Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2017, respectively. Qiang is now attached to the Centre for Computational Statistics and Machine Learning of Department of Computer Science, Univeristy College London .

Qiang's research interest mainly lies in data science and machine learning methods. He had experience in wearable computing like physiological signals processing. Curretly he focuses on deep learning with application on natural language processing and information retrieval. Feel free to contact him by email.
